Data for Vignette #2 re: Monthly rates of lion-hyena interactions correlate with relative lion abundance.

Monthly counts of lion-hyena interactions in Talek West.

Observation: Observation ID
year: year of survey
month: month of survey
TotalLionHyenaInteractions: The number of lion-hyena interactions in this month of that year.
NumberOfSessions: The total number of sessions (standardized to have a mean of 0 and a standard deviation of 1).
